Voskopoje

Located in southeastern Albania, Voskopoja is a historic mountain village known for its stone houses, ancient churches, and peaceful alpine atmosphere. Surrounded by forests and hills, it’s a perfect destination for culture lovers, nature walks, and winter escapes.

Activities

Winter Snow Activities

During winter, Voskopoja turns into a snowy retreat. Visitors can enjoy snow walks, sledding, and relaxed winter hikes through white-covered forests.

Nature Walks & Hiking

The area offers calm trails through pine forests and open landscapes, ideal for walking, light hiking, and breathing in fresh mountain air.

Restaurants & Hotels

Vila 118 Guest House

A cozy hotel offering warm rooms and a traditional atmosphere. Guests can relax by the fireplace and enjoy local food, especially popular during winter.

Vila Falo

A traditional restaurant serving local Albanian dishes made with fresh, regional ingredients. Known for its welcoming staff and rustic village setting.
