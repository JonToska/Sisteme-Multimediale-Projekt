Sarandë

A vibrant coastal city on the Ionian Sea, known for crystal-clear waters, beaches, and nearby ancient ruins.

Main Attractions

Ksamil Islands

Just a short drive from Sarandë, Ksamil is famous for its white sandy beaches and turquoise waters, often compared to tropical destinations.

Butrint National Park

A UNESCO World Heritage Site featuring ancient Greek and Roman ruins surrounded by lush nature, offering a unique blend of history and scenery.

Recommended Restaurants

Haxhi

One of Sarandë’s most popular seafood restaurants, offering freshly caught fish and traditional coastal dishes.

Fish Filipi

A cozy seaside taverna known for grilled seafood and relaxed atmosphere, perfect after a day at the beach.
