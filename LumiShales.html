Lumi i Shales

Lumi i Shalës is a crystal-clear mountain river in northern Albania, famous for its turquoise waters, dramatic canyon views, and untouched natural beauty.

Activities

Kayaking

Kayaking on Lumi i Shalës lets you explore the river up close. Paddle through calm sections with a friend, enjoy the crystal-clear water beneath you, and feel the fresh mountain air. Some parts have light currents, making it fun even for beginners.

Swimming & Cliff Jumping

The river is famous for its deep turquoise pools. Visitors can swim in the icy water or jump from safe rock spots into the river. It’s refreshing, exciting, and one of the most popular things to do here.

Hiking & Nature Walks

Short hikes along the river and surrounding hills offer peaceful walks, beautiful viewpoints, and plenty of photo spots. The trails are simple and perfect for exploring untouched nature.

Koman Lake Boat Tour

The journey to Lumi i Shalës is done by boat through Lake Koman, offering breathtaking views of cliffs, forests, and narrow passages. Many consider this one of the most beautiful boat rides in the Balkans.

Natural Highlights

Turquoise River & Canyons

Lumi i Shalës is known for its incredibly clear blue water and steep canyon walls. The contrast between the river and the mountains makes it one of Albania’s most photogenic locations.

Quiet & Untouched Environment

There are no big buildings or crowds. The area remains natural and calm, ideal for visitors looking to relax, disconnect, and enjoy silence and fresh air.
