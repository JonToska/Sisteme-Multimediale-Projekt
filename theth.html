Theth

Located in the Albanian Alps, Theth is a remote mountain village known for its dramatic landscapes, traditional stone houses, and untouched natural beauty, making it a perfect escape for nature lovers and hikers.

Main Attractions

Theth National Park

A protected alpine area known for dramatic mountains, deep valleys, and untouched nature. It’s perfect for hiking, exploring traditional villages, and experiencing the raw beauty of the Albanian Alps.

Grunas Waterfall

One of the most impressive waterfalls in northern Albania, falling from high rocky cliffs into a scenic canyon. A short hike leads to this powerful natural sight, especially stunning in spring and summer.

Blue Eye of Kaprre

A crystal-clear natural spring with deep blue and turquoise tones. Surrounded by forest and rock formations, it’s a peaceful spot ideal for relaxing, swimming, and photography.

Restaurants

Restaurant Freskia

A traditional restaurant offering homemade Albanian dishes made with fresh local ingredients. Visitors can enjoy hearty meals in a cozy, mountain-style setting after a long day of hiking.

Hotel Restaurant Alpet Theth

Known for its warm hospitality and generous portions, this restaurant serves classic northern Albanian food, including fresh dairy products, bread, and grilled meats.
